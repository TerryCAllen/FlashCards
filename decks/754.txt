7 Virtues of a Warrior (Bushido):Rectitude\n Courage \n Benevolence \n Respect\n Loyalty \n Honesty / Sincerity \n Honor
Rectitude:Doing what is right w/o regard for the consequences 
Courage: Inner strength to resist opposition\n Not just to step on the mat, but to make the right decisions 
Benevolence: Mindset to do good \n Be kind and generous
Respect: Politeness / Propriety\n Proper consideration and courtesy to others \n Don’t be an asshole 
Loyalty;Faithfulness & Alliance,\n not blind allegiance 
Honesty / Sincerity: Be a man of your word\n Do what you say you will\n Genuine Integrity in character & action 
Honor: Profound respect & ethical conduct
