Origins of Jiu Jitsu ; Trace back to India and China, then Japan approximately 400 years ago.

Who moved to Brazil from Scotland in 1801? ; George Gracie.

Who was Gustavo Gracie? ; A diplomat and descendant of George Gracie.

Who did Gustavo Gracie help bring to Brazil? ; Mitsuyo Maeda.

When did Mitsuyo Maeda come to Brazil? ; Early 1900s.

Who did Mitsuyo Maeda teach? ; Carlos Gracie.

Who was Carlos Gracie? ; Oldest of the five Gracie brothers.

Where did Carlos Gracie begin teaching Jiu Jitsu? ; Rio de Janeiro.

Who was Helio Gracie? ; Youngest Gracie brother.

What physical challenge did Helio Gracie face? ; He was physically frail and weighed about 140 lbs as an adult.

Why did Helio initially learn by observation? ; Doctors advised him not to engage in physical activity.

How did Helio learn Jiu Jitsu at first? ; By watching and memorizing Carlos Gracie's techniques.

How did Helio begin teaching? ; He substituted for Carlos when a student arrived and Carlos was late.

What did Helio do when techniques were difficult due to his size? ; Adapted them through leverage and efficiency.

What martial art evolved from Helio's adaptations? ; Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ).

Why did Helio's approach stand out? ; Courage and flawless technique.
